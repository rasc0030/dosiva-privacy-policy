Privacy Policy — Dosiva

Last updated: April 2026

Summary: Dosiva stores all your data exclusively on your device. No information is sent to external servers, collected by third parties, or shared with anyone.

1. Data collected

Dosiva allows you to record health information for your personal use, including:

  • Medications, dosages, schedules, and stock levels
  • Medication intake records
  • Blood glucose (glycemia) measurements
  • Insulin injection sites
  • Notification settings and app preferences

All of this data is entered voluntarily by you and exists solely to help you manage your medication routine.

2. Storage

Your data is stored exclusively on your device, in a local database (SQLite). Dosiva has no servers, does not transmit data over the internet, and does not use cloud services.

If you uninstall the app, your data will be permanently deleted — unless you have manually created a backup using the app's export function.

3. Data sharing

Dosiva does not share your data with third parties. No information is sent to advertisers, business partners, analytics services, or any other entity.

The app includes a PDF report export and backup function, which you may use voluntarily to share information with your doctor or save a backup copy. These actions are always initiated by you.

4. Device permissions

Dosiva requests the following Android permissions, all necessary for its operation:

  • Notifications: To send medication reminders at the configured times
  • Exact alarm: To ensure reminders are triggered at the correct time
  • Vibration: For notification alerts
  • Battery optimization: To request that Android does not restrict background reminders
  • Boot on startup: To automatically reschedule reminders after the phone restarts

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data.

5. Third-party services

Dosiva does not integrate third-party services such as analytics, tracking, advertising, or social login. The app works completely independently and offline.

6. Security

Since your data remains only on your device, security depends on your device's own protections (password, biometrics, system encryption). We recommend keeping your device protected with a password or biometrics.

7. Children's data

Dosiva is not intended for children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided data through the app, the data can be deleted by uninstalling the application.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through an app update. The date of the last update is indicated at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or about the Dosiva app, please contact us at: dosiva.app@gmail.com
